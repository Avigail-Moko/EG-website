So, who I am ...

Esther Green interior designer, Studio Alisa Sheinson.

Hi, I'm Esther Green, an artist and painter in the past.
4 years ago, I started doing interior design.
I graduated from architecture and interior design studies at Prog College.

My First Steps

I took my first steps in the world of interior design with the designer Yaron Yiftach Ben-Gera.
There I gained knowledge and experience in drawing, interior design, and especially planning solutions.
Today I am a freelancer in the field of interiors as well as an architect and interior designer at Studio Alisa Sheinson.

Among my major projects are designing children's rooms, gardens, hotel rooms, bathrooms, etc.

My Faith

I believe that every imagination can become a reality.
With great ambition and careful planning, down to the smallest details,
while emphasizing the customer's needs
and with, of course, special design, the sky's the limit.

My love is art, my specialty is designing, and my tool is planning.
